22 August, 2012 - Phantoms Esports is Back! New website on 25 August!



After a long span of time without a new website for our lovely fans to visit, we are now glad to welcome all of you to our brand new website!

We hope that the fans enjoy this website and keeps in touch with Phantoms, because we will have all the updated information about the organization and exciting initiatives for the fans. In our new home, you will be able to access tons of information regarding our teams, like results and awards, also you will have chance to see great blogs from our players and also share information through our forum (which is not yet available).

While at our website, Phantoms will present our users with reviews and rubrics, regarding our players and tournaments we might participate in and some giveaways whenever possible! This website marks the beginning of a completely new era, where you can expect the best from us.



As you can see, our/yours website is an BETA release, and as such, many features like the forums, player blogs and some other features will be released during the next weeks and months as soon as they are done. In the mean time you can send us feedback about bugs and maybe some features you would like to see in the new Phantoms website at feedback@phantoms-esports.com.

We managed to recover over 1300 user registrations from the old website, if you don't remember your old password please head over to our Password Recover page.

Stay tuned, the new Phantoms have arrived!
